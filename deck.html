Gigabox · 2026
The product

A neighbourhood store that also delivers in 60 minutes

What it is

Gigabox sells kitchen & sports goods online and from a real store in Jodhpur. Order before 9:30 PM, get it in an hour.

The edge

Unlike app-only rivals, there's a physical shop you can walk into — the trust asset most quick-commerce brands don't have.

The catch

She arrives at night, store nearly closed, on a first visit with no account, no history — and a bad memory of online shopping.

Gigabox · Question 3
09
After hours · 11:18 PM

When the store sleeps, the cart doesn't

The graceful close
  • ☾Honest header — “Delivery resumes 7 AM,” never a dead “unavailable” wall.
  • 🛒Cart stays open — she can still browse and build it at midnight.
  • ⏰Schedule for the morning in two taps; pay at the door.

A bounce at 11 PM is a lost user. A scheduled order is a won one.

Gigabox · Scheduling
10
Pitching the stores

Online and in-store, one promise

The store isn't a footnote — it's a conversion engine for the hesitant. We make “see it, then buy it” a first-class path.

  • 🛍️See it. Touch it. Then decide. Free demos in-store remove first-order fear.
  • 🏷️One price, both channels — kills the “online is a trick” suspicion.
  • ↩️Returns at the counter — a human to hand it back to.
  • 📍Store finder + “coming soon” — the brand feels like it's growing roots.
